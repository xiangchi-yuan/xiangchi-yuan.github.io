Research

My research interests revolve around multimodal representation learning, trustworthy representation learning, and their practical applications. Specifically, I'm currently fascinated by the potential of finetuning large pre-trained models with sparsity, which can enhance their trustworthiness while also reducing their computational costs.

I owe many thanks to all the brilliant co-authors!
